Frequently Asked Questions

Q. Where are the photos I select stored?

Selected photos and the generated collage are processed only on your iPhone. Nothing is uploaded to our servers. Generated collages are saved to your photo library only when you tap "Save to Photos".

Q. Can I change the output aspect ratio?

Yes. After tapping "Save / Share as one image", a preview screen appears with a segmented picker at the top: 1:1 (square), 4:5 (Instagram portrait), or 9:16 (Stories / Reels).

Q. Can I remove ads?

The app is free and supported by banner ads, so an ad-free option is not available. To opt out of personalized advertising, go to iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking and turn off Hunt9.

Q. How many theme colors are available?

Nine: Blue, Red, Yellow, Green, Purple, Pink, Orange, White, Black. Tap the colored circle in the top left to switch.

Q. The app isn't working correctly. What should I do?

Try fully quitting and relaunching the app, or update iOS and Hunt9 to the latest versions. If the issue persists, please contact us at the email above.
